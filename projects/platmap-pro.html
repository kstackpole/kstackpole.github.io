Work with me

I’m a multidisciplinary developer and designer with a passion for building tools and experiences that are both functional and fun. I thrive where creativity meets code.

I grew up in the Denver metro area and later moved to Atlanta, Georgia, to study Digital Media. My time in the Southeast was spent honing my craft in music and media, where I discovered a love for the digital canvas. Now, I’ve brought that passion back to the Mile High City, blending tech and design to build thoughtful, engaging experiences.

Today, I bring a full-stack mindset to every project, with fluency across design systems, front-end development, and automation tools. Whether I’m streamlining workflows or shaping intuitive user experiences, I’m always aiming to build things that are thoughtful, efficient, and just a little unexpected.
